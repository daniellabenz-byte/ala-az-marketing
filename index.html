Method

Source: Salesforce production, queried July 16, 2026. Arizona records identified by the 45 Arizona campus values on the lead's school-interest field, grouped into 20 campus families; tours matched by campus-code prefix on scheduled-tour records. "This school year" = August 1, 2025 onward. Lead and application figures include only records with system-recorded marketing attribution (a Meta lead ID, Google click ID, Pardot form conversion, or campaign tag); unattributed records are excluded entirely, and district-wide application totals live in the student information system. Attribution categories are mutually exclusive, assigned by strongest available proof (Meta lead ID → Google click ID → Pardot conversion → campaign tags); monthly counts use the application-date field. Leads interested in multiple campuses are counted once in totals.
